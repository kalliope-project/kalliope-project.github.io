Kalliope is a modular always-on voice controlled personal assistant designed for home automation.

Take a look to the community brain if you want to see more examples of usage, videos in other languages and also the configuration of Kalliope to get this result.

See the marketplace to have an idea of what you can do with Kalliope!

Kalliope can be installed on multiple linux system, and also on small single-board computers like the Raspberry Pi.
